ABOUT ME



Hey, I'm Catherine!


Welcome to my portfolio! I am a third year, graduating Cognitive Science Major with a specialization in Design and Interaction.

My design journey started a few quarters into my first year as an undergraduate and I have learned so much through my courses and projects.
